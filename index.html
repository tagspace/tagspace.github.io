RIP Top Tournament

We regret to inform you that we are shutting down Top Tournament.

Over the years Top Tournament has been used by some of the biggest and best universities across the UK (including Edinburgh, Loughborough, Nottingham, Birmingham, Warwick, etc.), and has been used to run countless tournaments across the world.

We are proud of what we have built and have been blown away by the incredible tournaments that we have seen you run.

Unfortunately, we cannot continue to maintain Top Tournament and it is time to say goodbye.

Your existing tournaments will continue to exist for a few more months, but you are no longer able to create new tournaments, and eventually we will shut down the service completely.

Stay safe and stay healthy during these difficult times. And thank you for your understanding.

Yours sincerely,

Tom and Johann
